Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Information we collect
  • Account information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials.
  • Usage data: We collect data about how you use Cooture — templates generated, features accessed, and session duration.
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, IP address, and timezone.
  • Payment information: Processed by our payment provider (Stripe). We never store raw card numbers.
2. How we use your information
  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To process transactions and send billing notifications.
  • To send product updates and security alerts (you can opt out anytime).
  • To detect and prevent fraud and abuse.
3. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Service providers who assist us in operating Cooture (e.g. Firebase, Stripe, Vercel).
  • Law enforcement when legally required.
4. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us at hello@cooture.dev.

5. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

6. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at hello@cooture.dev.

Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Cooture uses cookies and similar tracking technologies.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help websites remember your preferences and track usage patterns.

2. Cookies we use
  • Essential cookies: Required for authentication, session management, and security. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how users interact with Cooture (e.g. which features are most used). You can opt out.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your theme choice (light/dark), language, and other settings.
3. Third-party cookies

We may use third-party services (such as Google Analytics or Firebase) that set their own cookies. We do not control these cookies.

4. Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may prevent Cooture from functioning correctly.
